There are those that seek fortune, there are those that seek fame,

There are those that seek love, But which is your game.

Fortunes can be lost, fame can be a trap,

True love is guaranteed, it comes with a map.

You start the journey alone in the middle of the road,

Your burdens your own, no-one shares your load.

Your path's do cross somewhere down life's track,

From that moment on you need not look back.

Your true partner will take you for whoever you are,

Never seeing fault or flaw, not even a scar.

For love goes much deeper than body or skin,

True love is a feeling that glows from within.
