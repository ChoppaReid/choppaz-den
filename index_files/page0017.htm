You feel my presence as I come near,
Your heart fills with passion while your mind fills with fear.
My presence filled with charm, mystery and grace,
Your lustful thoughts now clear on your face.
As I approach you can't move or speak,
My touch you long for as your knees go weak.
My arms drape around you as you pucker your lips,
Your skin now tingling at my fingertips.
My eyes entrance you as closer we come,
Your body now wanting we two to be one.
I lean toward you, your eyes now afire,
Burning intensely with passion and desire.
I kiss down your arm and your pulse grows quick,
A small nip on your wrist, your blood I lick.
One split second of pain, you now feel pure bliss,
Power in your eyes, this look I can't miss.
You gasp for air as your heart starts to race,
Eternal life you are about to face.
To complete the circle I offer thyself to thee,
If you take that bite, you'll live for eternity.
Your teeth now sharpen, and your breath is now fast,
You can now make pain a thing of your past.
You pierce my neck and sip from my well,
Now feeling superior in each and every cell.
I see in your eyes your power you ponder,
Like a child set free you are now filled with wonder.
You absorb my knowledge like paper does water,
Now as my lover, I train you like daughter.
Your knowledge pool grows by the second so fast,
Each second you learn, smarter than the last.
As dawn now approaches we head underground,
Your strength and your speed do truly astound.
Your heart fills with sorrow due to fear of daylight,
But sorrow soon passes when you learn to take flight.
Without word now I teach you of your new given right,
Of the joys to be had as a creature of the night.
Your smile returns wicked and your eyes burn so bright,
Finding your first victim in the hunt you delight.
Removing his pain, you make your first strike,
Of your new power you truly do like.
We feast by the moon on your first found kill,
The power you feel truly does thrill.
