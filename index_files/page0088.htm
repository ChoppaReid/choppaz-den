Daylight does come as it does go,

Each moment passing like the falling snow.

Every second falls like snow to the ground,

Memories fade like the snow all around.

Sometimes like winter the memories are cold,

Other memories like summer you wish to hold.

Feelings change as time does it's dance,

Just like your vision can change at a glance.
