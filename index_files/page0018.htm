The time has come for your first true test,
Dracula has ordered your loyalty you attest.
A clan of mortals hunt us nearby,
Our lord has ordered they all must die.
He has heard of your talent and your lust for the kill,
He admires your talent, exuberance and skill.
Our ruler has ordered you lead the attack,
A dozen assailants you may choose from our pack.
Make your choice wisely with no favour or jest,
Our victory or failure your skill will attest.
No excuse for failure will Dracula accept,
All will be left in the sun whose acts are inept.
In one dark night the task must be done,
All of them killed, not leaving a one.
I leave you now to ponder your choice,
Upon your victory we all will rejoice.
You don't want to fail as our lord's chosen one,
For those who fail will feel the rays of the sun.

Onward march!
